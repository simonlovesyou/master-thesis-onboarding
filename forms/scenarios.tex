\section{Pre scenarios}
The test is to be conducted in the following steps
\begin{enumerate}
  \item Ask them questions from the pre-interview survey
  \item Inform the test participant of the mobileapp
  \item Let them fill out the PEU-PU survey while you setup the test environment
  \item Let them perform scenarios using the prototype
  \item After each scenario, ask them questions from the Post-scenario questionnaire
  \item Ask them questions from post-test survey and PEU-PU survey
\end{enumerate}
